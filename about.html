About

My name is Augustinas Naslenas, I'm a freelance graphic artist and illustrator currently working out of Sheffield UK. Story telling and visual art have always been passions for as long as I can remember myself. My Dad told me he had started teaching me how to draw when I was only 4.

In my late teens I became interested in Jungian psychology , Occult and magic traditions of various cultures. This involvement has been influencing my creative work since then and continues to do so.

In 2015 I went through a difficult transformative initiatory experience. The Dream Reaper character was born in the fusion of this experience and the knowledge I had collected previously. The character quickly claimed his own life and expanded into different mediums and art forms such as performance, music, video,illustrated writing.

The Dream Reaper - Escape from Soul City is the first one in a series of short stories based on dreams I have been having. This story took a form of a work in progress web comic. I decided to give you a middle segment of the story first and then add updates to the beginning and end. This way you will find not just what happened 'next' but also, what happened 'before'.

I hope you enjoy it!
